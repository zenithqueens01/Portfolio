Our Beginning

A legacy of learning that started from humble roots.

How We Started

Founded in 1995, Lumina School began with just two classrooms and a handful of dedicated educators. Driven by a passion to provide world-class education, the founding members built a foundation based on discipline, academic rigor, and character development.

Over the decades, we have expanded into a state-of-the-art campus, remaining true to our core belief that every child deserves an environment where their potential can truly flourish.

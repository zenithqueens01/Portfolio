Our Beginning

A legacy of learning that started from humble roots.

How We Started

Kings & Queenies Zenith Schools was founded in June 2017 with the vision of providing accessible and quality education through a free summer coaching program for pupils within the community, while full academic activities officially commenced in September 2017. The institution began with the Basic School section and subsequently expanded into the College section, reflecting steady growth driven by dedication, excellence, and God’s grace.

Today, the school operates successfully within two communities in Ikorodu, with its Headquarters located at Agunfoye Road, Adamo, and a Branch Campus at Olorunda, Araro, Adamo, Ikorodu. Since inception, the school has remained committed to delivering a well-rounded educational experience that combines academic excellence, moral discipline, leadership development, innovation, and practical skill acquisition. Kings & Queenies Zenith Schools has established a reputation for nurturing confident, responsible, and future-ready students through both classroom learning and experiential education. The school regularly organizes educational excursions and academic expeditions to reputable institutions and locations including Murtala Muhammed International Airport, Bigi Production Plant, Silverbird Galleria, Shoprite, Ikeja Airport Hotel Swimming Facility, and Origin Garden and Resort Museum and Zoo, among others, to broaden students’ exposure and enhance practical learning. The institution has consistently excelled in interschool competitions and has earned numerous awards and recognitions within and beyond Ikorodu in sports, debates, cultural presentations, Bible quiz competitions, and other academic activities. In line with its commitment to holistic education and youth empowerment, the school also offers vocational training and certification programs in diverse fields such as fashion designing, catering services, haircutting, hairdressing and cosmetology, shoe making, arts and crafts, and other entrepreneurial skill programs designed to equip students with valuable lifelong competencies for self-development and global relevance.
